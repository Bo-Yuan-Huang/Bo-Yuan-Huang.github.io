I am a research scientist at Intel Offensive Security Research. My work focuses on design methodology and automated verification of digital computing systems. A central theme of my research is leveraging formal methods (especially in modeling, verification, and synthesis) to ensure system correctness and security. Before joining Intel, I received my B.S. from National Taiwan University and my M.A. and Ph.D. from Princeton University.
