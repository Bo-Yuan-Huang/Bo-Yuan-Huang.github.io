I'm a PhD candidate in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Princeton University working with Prof. Sharad Malik. My research interest is applying formal methods in accelerator-rich computing platforms, especially software/hardware co-verification and hardware abstraction. I'm also interested in security related system verification. My current focus is on studying instruction-level abstraction based SoC firmware verification.

Before coming to Princeton, I received my BS in Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan University.
